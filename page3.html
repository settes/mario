About Mario Mendes


The Entrepreneur Side

He also held strategic positions in important and consolidated companies in Brazil, such as: MASTERAF BRASIL S / A (until 2011), now incorporated by THOMSON REUTERS, working on the development and launch of the first software offering as services (SaaS) in the distribution model in Cloud Computing for the fiscal location systems market in Latin America, integrating with the main business management systems currently in production in Brazil: ORACLE, MICROSOFT and SAP.

Mário Mendes was also responsible for the design and construction of the project approved by the FINEP economic grant in 2008 for MASTERSAF - TSL (TECNOLOGIA PARA SISTEMAS DE LEGISLATION S / A) in a national selection of innovative projects via the main innovation financing agency of Brazil , called the ON-LINE AND REAL TIME FISCAL AUTHORIZATION NOTE PLATFORM, COVERING GENERATION, PRINTING, VALIDATION, OIL AND ELECTRONIC ANTICIPATION.

Mario Mendes also closely followed the creation of the public digital bookkeeping system in Brazil (SPED) is one of the main responsible for popularizing the Software as a Service (SaaS) model in the country during its time in MASTERSAF, absolute market leader in its segment and now making OLITEL BRASIL S / A as the world's largest company based on AVAYA technology in the cloud computing services model. (SaaS and PaaS).


Innovation to save lives

Mario Mendes is also the founder, founder and builder of the platform based on Artificial Intelligence DR. WILSON, whose focus is on reducing the number of neglected diseases worldwide through cell phones. Dr. Wilson is currently the largest number of question and answer platform in the world within Google Assistant and was chosen by Bots Brazil as the best virtual assistant platform in the country.

The Dr. Wilson platform is also the only one in the world focused on health with Speech to Text Technology (STT), which can transform users' speech into text, and is submitted to an Artificial Intelligence platform that assesses the context and the user's intention, so that he can present a coherent answer, maintaining an objective and intelligent conversation.


Social Innovation

Mario Mendes is a specialist in underdeveloped and emerging markets. In 2014, he published the book on economics called "A Young Negro Wanted to Save the Planet," which describes potential markets in some Sub-Saharan African countries through an in-depth analysis of their vast natural resources and human capital.

His experiences in underdeveloped countries, coupled with practical experience and curiosity in clinical engineering, also allowed the young Mario Mendes in 2004 to idealize and construct some innovations for the blood components transport segment, creating a new market of transport equipment based on peltier air-conditioner for distribution logistics of blood, plasma and large vaccination companies. This model is still copied by manufacturers around the world in the pre-analytical logistics clinical analysis stage.

Mario Mendes has this stage of his life as the great vector of technological entrepreneurship that moves him.
